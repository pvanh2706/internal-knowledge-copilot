# Pilot Onboarding Guide

Tai lieu nay dung de gioi thieu nhanh cho cac team tham gia pilot Internal Knowledge Copilot.

## 1. Muc tieu pilot

Pilot nay dung de kiem chung xem he thong co giup team:

- Tim cau tra loi noi bo nhanh hon.
- Giam viec hoi dap lap lai.
- Onboarding nhan su moi tot hon.
- Chuan hoa tai lieu thanh wiki noi bo.
- Kiem soat viec AI tra loi dua tren nguon nao.

Day la giai doan thu nghiem co kiem soat, chua phai trien khai chinh thuc cho toan cong ty.

## 2. Ai tham gia

Moi team pilot can co:

- Pilot owner: nguoi theo doi tien do pilot va tong hop ket qua.
- Reviewer: nguoi duyet tai lieu, xu ly feedback sai, publish wiki.
- User: nguoi upload tai lieu, dat cau hoi, gui feedback.
- Admin/IT support: nguoi tao user, phan quyen, ho tro loi ky thuat.

## 3. He thong dung de lam gi

Trong pilot, he thong dung de:

- Luu tai lieu noi bo quan trong.
- Duyet tai lieu truoc khi dua vao kho tri thuc AI.
- Hoi dap bang tieng Viet tren tai lieu da duyet.
- Xem nguon trich dan cua cau tra loi.
- Feedback khi AI tra loi dung hoac sai.
- Tao wiki noi bo tu tai lieu goc.
- Theo doi KPI va audit log co ban.

## 4. He thong khong dung de lam gi trong pilot

Khong dung pilot cho:

- Luu tai lieu mat, nhay cam cao, hoac chua duoc phep chia se.
- Thay the hoan toan quy trinh phe duyet chinh thuc cua cong ty.
- Dua ra quyet dinh phap ly, nhan su, tai chinh hoac bao mat chi dua vao AI.
- Upload file scan anh chat luong kem neu chua co OCR.
- Trien khai dai tra cho toan cong ty khi chua danh gia ket qua pilot.

## 5. Cach tham gia hieu qua

- Hoi nhung cau hoi thuc te trong cong viec hang ngay.
- Doc nguon citation truoc khi tin vao cau tra loi quan trong.
- Danh dau Correct neu cau tra loi dung va huu ich.
- Danh dau Incorrect neu cau tra loi sai, thieu nguon, hoac khong dung y.
- Ghi chu ro khi feedback sai de reviewer biet can sua gi.
- Bao loi phan quyen neu khong thay tai lieu minh duoc phep xem.

## 6. Tieu chi thanh cong cua pilot

Pilot co tin hieu tot neu:

- User thuc su dat cau hoi trong cong viec hang ngay.
- Cau tra loi co nguon va co the kiem chung.
- Ty le feedback sai o muc chap nhan duoc va giam dan.
- Reviewer xu ly duoc feedback sai.
- Co wiki published tu cac tai lieu hay duoc hoi.
- Team thay viec tim thong tin nhanh hon cach cu.

## 7. Ket qua mong doi sau pilot

Sau pilot, team se tong hop:

- So user tham gia.
- So cau hoi AI.
- So tai lieu approved.
- So wiki published.
- Ty le feedback dung/sai.
- Nhung cau tra loi AI huu ich.
- Nhung diem AI chua tot.
- De xuat co mo rong sang team khac hay khong.
