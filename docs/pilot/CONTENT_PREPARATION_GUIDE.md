# Content Preparation Guide

Tai lieu nay giup cac team chuan bi tai lieu truoc khi dua vao pilot.

## 1. Muc tieu chuan bi noi dung

Noi dung dau vao cang ro thi AI tra loi cang co ich. Pilot nen bat dau voi bo tai lieu nho, dung, co owner va hay duoc hoi trong cong viec.

## 2. Loai tai lieu nen chon

Nen chon:

- FAQ noi bo.
- Quy trinh lam viec.
- Huong dan xu ly su co.
- Huong dan onboarding.
- Chinh sach noi bo trong pham vi team.
- Checklist van hanh.
- Tai lieu san pham/dich vu hay duoc hoi.

Uu tien tai lieu:

- Hay duoc hoi lap lai.
- Co noi dung ro rang.
- Con hieu luc.
- Co nguoi xac nhan dung sai.
- Co the chia se trong team pilot.

## 3. Loai tai lieu khong nen dua vao pilot dau

Khong nen chon:

- Tai lieu mat hoac nhay cam cao.
- Hop dong, thong tin phap ly nhay cam.
- Thong tin luong thuong/nhan su ca nhan.
- Tai lieu khach hang co du lieu ca nhan neu chua duoc phep.
- File scan anh khong OCR.
- Tai lieu qua cu, khong ai xac nhan.
- Tai lieu dang tranh cai noi dung.

## 4. Dinh dang file

He thong ho tro:

- PDF.
- DOCX.
- Markdown.
- TXT.

Khuyen nghi:

- Dung Markdown hoac DOCX neu co the.
- PDF nen la PDF co text, khong phai anh scan.
- TXT/Markdown nen co heading ro.
- Tach tai lieu qua dai thanh nhieu tai lieu theo chu de.

## 5. Cach dat ten file

Ten file nen ro nghia:

```text
refund-policy-v1.md
support-ticket-priority-guide.docx
new-employee-onboarding-checklist.pdf
```

Nen tranh:

```text
final.docx
new_final_2.pdf
abc.txt
quytrinh.docx
```

Neu co version, ghi version hoac ngay cap nhat trong title/noi dung tai lieu.

## 6. Cau truc tai lieu nen co

Tai lieu tot nen co:

- Tieu de ro.
- Muc dich.
- Pham vi ap dung.
- Doi tuong ap dung.
- Cac buoc thuc hien.
- Dieu kien/ngoai le.
- Nguoi hoac team phu trach.
- Ngay cap nhat.

Vi du cau truc:

```text
# Quy trinh xu ly ticket uu tien cao

## Muc dich

## Pham vi ap dung

## Dieu kien kich hoat

## Cac buoc thuc hien

## Thoi gian phan hoi

## Nguoi phu trach

## Luu y
```

## 7. Phan loai folder

Truoc khi upload, team nen thong nhat folder:

- Theo team: Support, Engineering, Operations.
- Theo chu de: Refund, Incident, Onboarding.
- Theo muc dich: FAQ, Process, Policy, Troubleshooting.

Khong nen:

- Tao folder qua sau.
- Dat mot tai lieu vao folder sai pham vi.
- Dung folder company-wide cho tai lieu chi cua mot team.

## 8. Checklist truoc khi upload

Truoc khi upload, kiem tra:

- Tai lieu con hieu luc.
- Co nguoi chiu trach nhiem noi dung.
- Khong chua du lieu cam upload.
- Dinh dang file duoc ho tro.
- Ten file ro nghia.
- Folder dung.
- Reviewer cua team da biet tai lieu nay se duoc upload.
