# Pilot Operating Playbook

Tai lieu nay mo ta cach van hanh pilot Internal Knowledge Copilot trong 2-4 tuan.

## 1. Nguyen tac van hanh

- Bat dau nho, do ket qua that.
- Chi dung tai lieu da duoc phep trong pilot.
- Moi team phai co reviewer.
- Moi feedback sai can co nguoi xem.
- Khong mo rong pham vi neu chua co KPI va bai hoc tu pilot.

## 2. Vai tro va trach nhiem

Pilot owner:

- Theo doi lich pilot.
- Dieu phoi reviewer, user va IT support.
- Tong hop KPI va feedback.
- Bao cao ket qua sau pilot.

Reviewer:

- Duyet/reject tai lieu.
- Xu ly feedback Incorrect.
- Generate va publish wiki khi can.
- De xuat tai lieu can bo sung.

User:

- Hoi dap trong cong viec thuc te.
- Upload tai lieu neu duoc phan cong.
- Gui feedback dung/sai.

Admin/IT support:

- Tao user/team/folder.
- Phan quyen.
- Ho tro loi dang nhap, quyen, he thong.
- Cung cap KPI ky thuat neu can.

## 3. Lich van hanh de xuat

Tuan 0 - Chuan bi:

- Chon team pilot.
- Chi dinh reviewer.
- Chon user tham gia.
- Chon 30-50 tai lieu dau tien.
- Tao folder va phan quyen.
- Upload va approve bo tai lieu mau.
- Chay demo noi bo voi reviewer.

Tuan 1 - Kickoff va su dung co huong dan:

- Gioi thieu muc tieu pilot cho user.
- Huong dan cach hoi AI va feedback.
- Theo doi loi dang nhap/quyen hang ngay.
- Reviewer xu ly feedback sai moi ngay.

Tuan 2 - Chuan hoa tri thuc:

- Xem top cau hoi va top cited sources.
- Generate wiki cho tai lieu hay duoc hoi.
- Publish wiki sau khi reviewer kiem tra.
- Ghi nhan cau hoi AI tra loi chua tot.

Tuan 3-4 - Danh gia:

- Tong hop KPI.
- Lay feedback user/reviewer.
- Xac dinh cai tien can lam.
- De xuat tiep tuc, mo rong, hay dung pilot.

## 4. Nhip hop goi y

Kickoff:

- 30-45 phut.
- Gioi thieu muc tieu, cach dung, quy tac du lieu.

Daily check trong tuan dau:

- 10-15 phut hoac cap nhat async.
- Tap trung vao loi dang nhap, phan quyen, cau tra loi sai.

Weekly review:

- 30 phut.
- Xem KPI, feedback sai, tai lieu can them, wiki can publish.

Final review:

- 45-60 phut.
- Tong hop ket qua va quyet dinh buoc tiep theo.

## 5. Quy trinh hang ngay

User:

- Dat cau hoi thuc te.
- Gui feedback dung/sai.
- Bao loi neu khong thay tai lieu.

Reviewer:

- Kiem tra document pending.
- Kiem tra feedback Incorrect.
- Xu ly nhung feedback quan trong.

Admin/IT:

- Kiem tra loi user/quyen.
- Dam bao he thong chay on dinh.

## 6. Quy trinh hang tuan

Pilot owner:

- Lay KPI tu dashboard.
- Tong hop feedback.
- Chon 3-5 van de noi bat.
- De xuat hanh dong tuan tiep theo.

Reviewer:

- Chon tai lieu can tao wiki.
- Xac nhan tai lieu can update.
- Danh dau feedback da xu ly.

## 7. Tieu chi dung pilot

Co the dung pilot neu:

- Phat hien rui ro du lieu nghiem trong.
- Khong co reviewer xu ly noi dung.
- User khong su dung thuc te.
- Chat luong cau tra loi qua thap va khong cai thien duoc bang tai lieu/wiki.

## 8. Tieu chi mo rong pilot

Co the mo rong neu:

- User dung thuc te va co feedback.
- Khong co loi phan quyen nghiem trong.
- Reviewer xu ly duoc queue.
- Dashboard cho thay nguon tri thuc duoc dung lap lai.
- Team dong y rang he thong giup tiet kiem thoi gian.
