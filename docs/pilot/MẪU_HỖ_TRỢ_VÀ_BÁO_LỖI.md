# Support And Issue Templates

Dung cac mau nay de bao loi hoac gui yeu cau trong pilot.

## 1. Mau bao loi dang nhap

```text
Loai yeu cau: Loi dang nhap
Ho ten:
Email:
Team:
Thoi diem gap loi:
Thong bao loi neu co:
Anh chup man hinh neu co:
Da thu doi trinh duyet/mang chua:
Ghi chu them:
```

## 2. Mau bao loi khong thay tai lieu

```text
Loai yeu cau: Khong thay tai lieu/folder
Ho ten:
Email:
Team:
Ten tai lieu/folder mong doi:
Ly do ban nghi minh nen co quyen:
Thoi diem phat hien:
Anh chup man hinh neu co:
Ghi chu them:
```

## 3. Mau bao cau tra loi AI sai

Nen dung feedback Incorrect trong he thong truoc. Neu can bao rieng, dung mau nay:

```text
Loai yeu cau: Cau tra loi AI sai
Ho ten:
Email:
Cau hoi da dat:
Cau tra loi AI:
Nguon/citation AI dua ra:
Vi sao cau tra loi sai hoac thieu:
Tai lieu dung neu ban biet:
Muc do anh huong: Thap / Trung binh / Cao
Ghi chu them:
```

## 4. Mau yeu cau cap quyen

```text
Loai yeu cau: Cap quyen
Ho ten:
Email:
Team:
Folder/tai lieu can truy cap:
Ly do can truy cap:
Thoi han can truy cap neu co:
Nguoi phe duyet neu co:
```

## 5. Mau de xuat them tai lieu

```text
Loai yeu cau: De xuat them tai lieu
Team:
Ten tai lieu:
Chu de:
Tai lieu nay giup tra loi cau hoi nao:
Nguoi owner/reviewer noi dung:
Muc do uu tien: Thap / Trung binh / Cao
Ghi chu them:
```

## 6. Mau de xuat tao wiki

```text
Loai yeu cau: De xuat tao wiki
Team:
Tai lieu nguon:
Chu de wiki de xuat:
Vi sao can wiki:
Cau hoi nao nen duoc wiki nay tra loi:
Reviewer phu trach:
Ghi chu them:
```

## 7. Mau bao loi he thong

```text
Loai yeu cau: Loi he thong
Ho ten:
Email:
Trang/man hinh gap loi:
Hanh dong dang thuc hien:
Thoi diem gap loi:
Thong bao loi neu co:
Anh chup man hinh:
Loi co lap lai duoc khong:
Ghi chu them:
```
