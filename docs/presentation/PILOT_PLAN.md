# Pilot plan - Internal Knowledge Copilot

## 1. Muc tieu pilot

Kiem chung xem Internal Knowledge Copilot co giup cac team tim kiem, hoi dap va chuan hoa tri thuc noi bo nhanh hon khong, trong khi van dam bao quyen truy cap va quy trinh reviewer.

## 2. Pham vi de xuat

Thoi gian:

- 2-4 tuan.

Team tham gia:

- 1-2 team dau tien.
- Goi y: Support, Ky thuat, Van hanh, CSKH hoac team co nhieu tai lieu quy trinh.

Du lieu:

- 30-50 tai lieu noi bo quan trong.
- Uu tien tai lieu hay duoc hoi lai, quy trinh lap lai, FAQ, huong dan xu ly su co, onboarding.

Nguoi tham gia:

- 1 owner pilot.
- 1 reviewer moi team.
- 5-15 user dung thu moi team.
- 1 nguoi ky thuat ho tro van hanh trong thoi gian pilot.

## 3. Tieu chi chon tai lieu

Nen chon:

- Tai lieu dang duoc dung thuc te.
- Tai lieu co noi dung hoi dap ro.
- Tai lieu phu hop de chia se trong team pilot.
- Tai lieu co owner/reviewer de xac nhan dung sai.

Khong nen chon o pilot dau:

- Tai lieu qua nhay cam.
- Tai lieu chua duoc phep chia se.
- Tai lieu qua cu hoac chua co nguoi xac nhan.
- File scan anh chat luong kem.

## 4. Tieu chi thanh cong

Pilot duoc xem la co tin hieu tot neu:

- User dat cau hoi thuc te moi tuan.
- Ty le cau tra loi bi danh dau sai o muc chap nhan duoc va giam dan sau khi reviewer xu ly.
- Reviewer publish duoc mot so wiki tu tai lieu goc.
- Dashboard cho thay co nguon tai lieu duoc dung lap lai.
- User danh gia viec tim thong tin nhanh hon cach cu.
- Khong phat sinh loi phan quyen nghiem trong.

## 5. KPI theo doi

- So cau hoi AI theo ngay/tuan.
- So user active trong pilot.
- So tai lieu upload.
- So tai lieu approved/rejected.
- So wiki draft generated.
- So wiki published.
- So feedback Correct/Incorrect.
- So incorrect feedback da xu ly.
- Top cited sources.
- Thoi gian trung binh tu upload den approve.

## 6. Lich pilot goi y

Tuan 0 - Chuan bi:

- Chon team pilot.
- Chon reviewer.
- Chon 30-50 tai lieu.
- Tao user/team/folder.
- Upload va approve bo tai lieu dau tien.
- Chay smoke test truoc khi demo cho user.

Tuan 1 - Bat dau dung thu:

- Huong dan user cach hoi AI va gui feedback.
- Theo doi loi dang nhap, quyen, upload, Q&A.
- Reviewer xu ly feedback sai dau tien.

Tuan 2 - Chuan hoa tri thuc:

- Generate wiki draft tu tai lieu duoc hoi nhieu.
- Publish wiki sau khi reviewer kiem tra.
- So sanh chat luong cau tra loi truoc/sau khi co wiki.

Tuan 3-4 - Danh gia mo rong:

- Tong hop KPI.
- Lay feedback cua user/reviewer.
- Lap danh sach cai tien can lam truoc khi mo rong.
- De xuat tiep tuc, mo rong, hay dung pilot.

## 7. Rui ro pilot va cach xu ly

Tai lieu dau vao kem:

- Bat dau voi bo tai lieu nho, co reviewer xac nhan.
- Uu tien tai lieu hay duoc hoi lai.

User hoi qua rong:

- Huong dan user chon scope folder/document khi can.
- Them FAQ mau trong buoi kickoff.

AI tra loi chua dung:

- Yeu cau user feedback Incorrect.
- Reviewer xu ly queue hang ngay trong tuan dau.
- Publish wiki cho nhung chu de hay hoi.

Thieu nguoi reviewer:

- Moi team pilot phai co reviewer ro rang truoc khi bat dau.
- Gioi han so tai lieu ban dau neu reviewer khong co nhieu thoi gian.

## 8. Ket qua can bao cao sau pilot

- Tong so user tham gia.
- Tong so cau hoi.
- Ty le feedback Correct/Incorrect.
- So feedback sai da xu ly.
- So wiki da publish.
- Top 10 nguon duoc hoi/trich dan nhieu.
- Cac tinh huong AI tra loi tot.
- Cac tinh huong AI tra loi chua tot.
- De xuat cai tien truoc khi mo rong.
- De xuat co/khong mo rong sang team tiep theo.
