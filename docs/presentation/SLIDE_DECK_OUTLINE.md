# Slide deck outline - Internal Knowledge Copilot

Day la khung 10 slide de dua vao PowerPoint/Google Slides.

## Slide 1 - Internal Knowledge Copilot

Subtitle: Thu nghiem tro ly tri thuc noi bo cho cac team trong cong ty

Noi dung ngan:

- Hoi dap tren tai lieu noi bo da duyet.
- Cau tra loi co nguon trich dan.
- Co reviewer kiem soat chat luong tri thuc.

Loi noi:

Hom nay em muon trinh bay mot MVP giup cong ty thu nghiem cach tap trung va khai thac tri thuc noi bo bang AI, nhung van co kiem soat quyen truy cap, nguon trich dan va reviewer.

## Slide 2 - Van de hien tai

Noi dung:

- Tai lieu rai rac, kho tim.
- Cau hoi noi bo bi lap lai.
- Nhan su moi onboarding cham.
- Kho biet tai lieu nao la ban dung.
- Dung AI khong co nguon se co rui ro.

Loi noi:

Diem dau khong nam o viec cong ty thieu tai lieu, ma la tai lieu kho tim, kho biet ban nao dung, va nhieu kien thuc van nam trong dau nguoi cu.

## Slide 3 - Muc tieu thu nghiem

Noi dung:

- Tap trung tai lieu quan trong.
- Hoi dap bang tieng Viet.
- Cau tra loi co citation.
- Chi dung tai lieu da approve.
- Tao wiki chuan hoa tu tai lieu goc.
- Do KPI va feedback trong pilot.

Loi noi:

Muc tieu la chay thu mot vong nho de xem AI co giup giam thoi gian tim tri thuc noi bo hay khong, dong thoi van giu duoc co che kiem duyet.

## Slide 4 - Giai phap MVP

Noi dung:

- User upload tai lieu.
- Reviewer approve/reject.
- AI Q&A co nguon.
- Feedback dung/sai.
- Reviewer xu ly feedback.
- Generate va publish wiki.
- Dashboard va audit log.

Loi noi:

MVP hien tai khong chi la chatbot. No co luong tai lieu, review, hoi dap, feedback va wiki de tri thuc duoc cai thien dan.

## Slide 5 - Cach he thong hoat dong

Noi dung dang flow:

```text
Tai lieu noi bo
-> Reviewer approve
-> Index vao kho tri thuc
-> User hoi AI
-> AI tra loi co nguon
-> User feedback
-> Reviewer cai thien wiki
```

Loi noi:

Tri thuc khong di thang tu file vao cau tra loi. No phai qua approve, co citation, co feedback, va co the duoc chuan hoa thanh wiki.

## Slide 6 - Demo nghiep vu

Noi dung:

- Upload tai lieu.
- Approve tai lieu.
- Hoi AI va xem citation.
- Gui feedback sai.
- Reviewer xem queue.
- Generate/publish wiki.
- Xem dashboard.

Loi noi:

Phan demo se di theo luong lam viec that cua team, khong demo code hay kien truc ky thuat.

## Slide 7 - Gia tri ky vong

Noi dung:

- Tim cau tra loi nhanh hon.
- Giam hoi dap lap lai.
- Onboarding nhanh hon.
- Tri thuc duoc chuan hoa.
- Co quyen truy cap va audit.
- Do duoc hieu qua bang KPI.

Loi noi:

Gia tri chinh la giam thoi gian tim va hoi lai tri thuc noi bo, dong thoi tao mot quy trinh chuan hoa tri thuc co nguoi chiu trach nhiem.

## Slide 8 - Pham vi pilot de xuat

Noi dung:

- 1-2 team dau tien.
- 30-50 tai lieu quan trong.
- 1 reviewer moi team.
- Chay 2-4 tuan.
- Review KPI hang tuan.

Loi noi:

Em de xuat bat dau nho de kiem chung gia tri that, khong dua ra toan cong ty ngay.

## Slide 9 - Rui ro va kiem soat

Noi dung:

- AI sai: citation, feedback, reviewer queue.
- Lo thong tin: phan quyen folder/team/user.
- Tai lieu chua duyet: khong dung cho AI.
- MVP chua production lon: pilot gioi han truoc.

Loi noi:

Nhung rui ro chinh da co co che kiem soat trong MVP. Pilot giup minh kiem chung tiep truoc khi mo rong.

## Slide 10 - De xuat quyet dinh

Noi dung:

- Phe duyet pilot.
- Chon team tham gia.
- Chi dinh reviewer.
- Chon bo tai lieu thu nghiem.
- Thong nhat KPI thanh cong.

Loi noi:

Quyet dinh em can hom nay la cho phep chay pilot co kiem soat voi mot vai team. Sau pilot, minh se co so lieu de quyet dinh co mo rong hay khong.
